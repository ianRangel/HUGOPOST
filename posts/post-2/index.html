Duotone Color Effect

October 5, 2017

The most trendy effect: Duotone


Theres no bigger trend effect in web and apps those days than the doutone color effect. Searching for new ways get customers attention, Spotify and New York firm Collins mixed 2 trends of Design to find a final result. The first one is vibrant colors, which were used to show emotion in various ways that music can be expresed. The second one is minimalism, where brings the limited ideas for color palettes. The result of this mix was duotone, which can be very easy to be created and creates a very powerful, beautiful and interesting effect.

Actually the duotone idea is not new, but Spotify is bringing notoriety and popularity to the effect, which just combine a pair of colors over a photo. THe visual is so trendy that it is easily found in lots of websites and companies trying to achieve the same fun communication with clients.

Duotone has a lot of positive points and brings benefits to visual material. The name comes from the use of two colors in printing process. Two colors or shades of same color or with black. Some benefits of duotone are: focal attention, contrast, increase readability when mixed wiht typography, works as background and easy to use.

Gradient Map Go to Layer > New Adjustment Layer > Gradient Map. Choose a Gradient from the Preset list, or create a custom one. Choose your color palette to make your duotone. You can make even more interesting, go to Layer > New Adjustment Layer > Brightness and Contrast.
