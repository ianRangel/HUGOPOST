Graphic design trends 2018: low poli design is still hot


Everybody knows that trends can be hot for months, years and suddenly go away. Its necessary always to be up-to-date. Researchs and browse websites are a good wat to know what trend will be in use for the next year. In graphic design there are some trends this year that generate a buzz around. In this post we will talk about a specific trend that is largely appreciated and very artistic one. The Low Poli.

Low Poli comes from Low Poligon. It was originally used for making 3D models and scenes for videogames

and in 2017 is part of almost all graphic design, movies (Game of thrones intro), fashion fields (shoes). Very well used on logos, posters, business cards, Low poli and your strong geometric shapes and colors creates a 3D perfection and sophisticated futurist style to be used in 2018.
